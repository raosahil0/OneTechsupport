## 🧠 RESEARCH DELIVERABLES

### 1. Target Audience Deep Dive

Create detailed personas for:

**Persona 1: SME Business Owner**
- Age range, job role, pain points
- What they value in an IT partner
- Decision-making triggers
- Websites they admire

**Persona 2: HR Manager**
- Age range, job role, pain points
- Staffing challenges they face
- What makes them choose a vendor
- Information they need before contacting

**Persona 3: IT Manager**
- Age range, job role, pain points
- Technical requirements they care about
- How they evaluate IT support providers
- Red flags they watch for

### 2. Competitive Analysis

Research and analyze:

**Direct Competitors (IT Staffing in India):**
- Find 3-5 competing agency websites
- List what they do well
- Identify their weaknesses
- Note missing opportunities

**Aspirational References (Awwards-Winning B2B):**
- Study: Stripe.com, Linear.app, Vercel.com
- Extract 5 design patterns to adapt
- Identify 3 interaction ideas to borrow
- Note 2 content strategies to emulate

### 3. Emotional Journey Mapping

Map the visitor's emotional journey:

**Awareness Stage:**
- What they're feeling
- What they need to see
- Content that resonates

**Consideration Stage:**
- What questions they have
- What proof they need
- How to build trust

**Decision Stage:**
- What pushes them to act
- What friction exists
- How to remove barriers

### 4. Theme Strategy Recommendation

Recommend a visual theme direction:

**Option A: "Modern Tech Confidence"**
- Colors: Deep Navy (#0A1628), Electric Blue (#3B82F6), Teal (#06B6D4)
- Mood: Trust, stability, innovation
- Best for: Corporate clients, enterprise deals

**Option B: "Bold Innovation"**
- Colors: Charcoal (#1E293B), Vibrant Blue (#2563EB), Amber (#F59E0B)
- Mood: Energy, action, results
- Best for: Startups, fast-growing companies

**Option C: "Minimal Sophistication"**
- Colors: Slate (#334155), Sky Blue (#0EA5E9), White space
- Mood: Premium, refined, understated
- Best for: High-value contracts, premium positioning

**Your Recommendation:**
- Pick one theme
- Explain why it fits this business
- Note any customizations for Indian market

### 5. Content Strategy

Define content priorities:

**Homepage:**
- Key message hierarchy
- Must-have sections
- Primary CTA

**Services Pages:**
- Information architecture
- Detail level needed
- Conversion path

**Social Proof:**
- Types of proof needed (testimonials, case studies, logos)
- How to present credibility
- Trust signals to highlight

### 6. Awwards-Winning Elements

List 10 specific elements that would make this site award-worthy:

Example:
1. Animated gradient hero background
2. Scroll-triggered service card reveals
3. Interactive stats counter animation
...

For each element, note:
- Where it appears
- Why it matters
- Implementation complexity (Low/Medium/High)

### 7. Success Metrics

Define what success looks like:

**Business Metrics:**
- Contact form submissions per month
- Time on page targets
- Bounce rate goals

**Technical Metrics:**
- Lighthouse score targets
- Core Web Vitals thresholds
- Load time goals

**Design Metrics:**
- Awwwards submission criteria
- Accessibility standards (WCAG 2.1 AA)
- Mobile responsiveness benchmarks
